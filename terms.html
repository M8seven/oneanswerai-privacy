Service Description

OneAnswerAI is an intelligent assistant that simultaneously queries OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Anthropic Claude, then fuses the responses into a single optimized answer.

Free Tier

All users receive 5 free text queries per day. The daily quota resets at midnight UTC. Media queries (images and documents) are not included in the free tier.

Subscriptions

OneAnswerAI offers the following auto-renewable subscription plans:

  • Weekly Pro ($5.99/week): 30 text queries/day, 3 media queries/day, access to additional query packs
  • Monthly Pro ($17.00/month): 40 text queries/day, 3 media queries/day, access to additional query packs

Payment: Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.

Renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

Management: You can manage or cancel your subscription in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.

Consumable Purchases

Query packs are one-time purchases that add credits to your account. Once credited, they are non-refundable. Available packs:

  • 10 Text Queries — $0.99
  • 50 Text Queries — $3.99
  • 100 Text Queries — $6.99
  • 5 Text + 1 Media Query — $1.99
  • 5 Media Queries — $1.99 (requires active subscription)
