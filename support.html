Frequently Asked Questions

How does OneAnswerAI work?

OneAnswerAI sends your question to three leading AI providers — OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Anthropic Claude — simultaneously. It then returns the best answer using one of two modes: Picking (selects the best single answer) or Meta-Fusion (synthesizes all responses into one comprehensive answer).

What AI providers are used?

OneAnswerAI uses OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini), Google Gemini (Gemini 2.0 Flash), and Anthropic Claude (Claude 3 Haiku). These are among the most advanced AI models available.

Is my data stored?

Your conversation history is stored only on your device. Queries are sent to AI providers for processing but are not permanently saved on our servers. We do not collect personal information, location data, or tracking data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iOS Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > OneAnswerAI > Cancel Subscription. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore purchases?

Open OneAnswerAI > Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions or unused query packs associated with your Apple ID.

What languages are supported?

OneAnswerAI is available in 7 languages: English, Italian, Spanish, Arabic, Chinese (Simplified), German, and French. The AI can respond in any language you write in.

Can I analyze PDF documents and images?

Yes! Subscribers can send PDF documents and images for AI analysis. Media files are processed temporarily and are not stored on our servers.
