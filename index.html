OneAnswerAI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Overview

OneAnswerAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Device Identifier: We generate a SHA256 hash of your device's unique identifier for authentication purposes. This hash is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Queries: Your questions are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. These queries are not stored on our servers.

Data Stored Locally

Conversation History: Your chat history is stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Analytics or tracking data

Third-Party Services

OpenAI: We use OpenAI's API to process your queries. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data processed by their services. Visit openai.com/privacy for details.

Data Security

All communications between the app and our servers use HTTPS encryption. Authentication tokens are stored securely in your device's Keychain.

Children's Privacy

OneAnswerAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
