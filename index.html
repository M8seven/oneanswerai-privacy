OneAnswerAI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Overview

OneAnswerAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Device Identifier: We generate a SHA256 hash of your device's unique identifier for authentication purposes. This hash is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Queries: Your questions are sent simultaneously to three AI providers: OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini), Google Gemini (Gemini 2.0 Flash), and Anthropic Claude (Claude 3 Haiku). The responses are fused into a single optimized answer. Queries are not permanently stored on our servers.

Server-Side Data

To manage quotas and subscriptions, we store the following data on our server (PostgreSQL hosted on Render):

  • Anonymous device hash
  • Subscription tier
  • Daily query counter
  • Purchased query credits
  • Subscription status and expiration date

No personally identifiable information is stored.

Data Stored Locally

Conversation History: Your chat history is stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

OneAnswerAI offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Weekly Pro at $5.99/week, Monthly Pro at $17.00/month) and consumable query packs. All payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Payment or financial information

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

  • OpenAI: AI query processing. openai.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini: AI query processing. ai.google.dev/terms
  • Anthropic Claude: AI query processing. anthropic.com/privacy
  • Render: Server hosting. render.com/privacy

Data Security

All communications between the app and our servers use HTTPS encryption. Authentication tokens are stored securely in your device's Keychain.

Children's Privacy

OneAnswerAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
